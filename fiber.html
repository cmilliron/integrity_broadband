Fiber Optic Patch Cords

IBP OpticalTM Patch Cords are constructed of quality materials proven in the industry to provide the reliability needed for your network. Integrity Broadband Products provides Patch Cords in any customizable Length, Connectorization, and Mating. Each IBP OpticalTM Patch Cord is optically tested for quality control.
