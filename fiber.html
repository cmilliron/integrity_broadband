Fiber Optic Quick Connectors

IBP OpticalTM Connectors are Quick Connect style SC or LC, Single Mode or Multi Mode connectors available in APC and UPC mating. Fast fiber optic connectors can provide a quick and easy termination of fibers in the field. Options are available for 900 micron allowing the installer or technician to terminate and make connections in minutes at the premise or fiber patch panel. This connector system removes any requirement for epoxy, adhesives, or costly curing ovens. Fiber Fast Connector is used for field assembled optical fiber connectorization widely used in Fiber To The Home (FTTH) applications to assemble with the drop cable quickly.

Options

  • SC or LC
  • APC or UPC
  • Single Mode or Multi Mode

Optical Properties

  • Insertion Loss: ≤ 0.3 dB
  • Return Loss: ≥ 45dB
  • Tensile Strength: F>50N
  • Operating Temperature: -20⁰ ~ +75⁰C
  • Storage Temperature: -40⁰ ~ +85⁰C
